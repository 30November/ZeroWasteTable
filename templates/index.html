Our Core Mandate

A Purpose-Driven Ecosystem Mitigating Hunger & Eco-Damage.

Global food waste accounts for approximately 8% to 10% of worldwide greenhouse gas emissions. Millions of tons of nutritious, edible surplus food are thrown away daily by food merchants and hospitality hubs while millions of local citizens suffer from food insecurity.

ZeroWasteTable leverages real-time digital matchmaking, predictive logistics maps, and visual dashboard analytics to orchestrate instant handoffs from food hubs to community soup kitchens and distribution groups.
